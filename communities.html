Overview

I am a Fan boy, but not an apple-y one. If you have not figured it out by this point, I love Google+ and there really are some great reasons why I choose to post most of my work there vs Facebook or any other social media site. Google+ has quickly become a haven for photographers of every variety, not only that but the amount of people looking for, and sharing great images far surpasses any other site I have found.

The next reason is because G+ is a community. Seriously if you want to find people that like what YOU like, photography or not, you can find it, share it, learn from it! I am a part of many communities and below I have listed a few of the Photography ones I am following.

Why Google+?

  • Now boasts the most profiles
  • Superior site for photography
  • Awesome helpful communities
  • Learn from serious pros
  • I am there! Duh
  • Cool way to field test
  • Meet great helpful people
